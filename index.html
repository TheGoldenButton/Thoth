The System of Thoth

The Mystical Framework

This engine acts as a digital mirror for esoteric divination, built on layers of established occult and astrological frameworks:

  • The Thoth Tarot: Rooted in the profound archetypal system of Aleister Crowley and Frieda Harris, treating the deck not merely as cards, but as living vectors of universal consciousness.
  • Qabalistic Pathworking: Each Major Arcana card maps directly to a specific Hebrew letter and a "Path" connecting the Sephiroth on the Tree of Life. The minor cards represent manifestations across the four Kabbalistic worlds (Atziluth, Briah, Yetzirah, Assiah).
  • Planetary Hours: The engine syncs to the Chaldean planetary hour of your current local time. The governing celestial body (e.g., Saturn for restriction, Venus for harmony) intrinsically flavors the resulting reading.
  • Elemental Dignities: Cards are dynamically evaluated based on their neighboring elemental affinities. Fire and Water weaken one another; Fire and Air empower one another. The engine uses this grid to amplify or shadow specific voices in the reading.

The Technical Engine

At its core, Thoth is a local, privacy-first topological inference system bridging deterministic code with generative AI:

  • Spatio-Temporal Entropy: Your precise location (if permitted), the exact millisecond of your query, and ongoing astrological cycles are calculated mathematically to seed a cryptographic pseudo-random number generator (PRNG), locking the draw specifically to your point in space-time.
  • Semantic Resonance: A custom topological Markov map runs in the browser, analyzing your text input and building a "conceptual gravity matrix". It weighs how heavily your chosen words resonate with the underlying esoteric metadata of the deck.
  • LLM Synthesis (Optional): Once the physical, temporal, and conceptual vectors align to cast the spread, you can optionally translate the raw symbols using a Local or Cloud LLM of your choice. The AI acts as the final channel, synthesizing the raw symbols into human meaning.
